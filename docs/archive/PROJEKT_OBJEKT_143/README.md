# PROJEKT_OBJEKT_143 archive

Last verified: 2026-05-09

This directory preserves the prose and supporting documents of the
PROJEKT_OBJEKT_143 operating ledger. The ledger ran from approximately
2026-05-04 to 2026-05-08, across the `cycle-2026-05-08-stabilizat-2-closeout`
release. It carried directive structure, success criteria, and evidence paths
for water (VODA), aviation (AVIATSIYA), communications (SVYAZ), user
experience (UX), stabilization (STABILIZAT), defects (DEFEKT), and design
(DIZAYN) work, and it sealed the live release on master at SHA `babae19` plus
the follow-up SHA `06a404b`.

The substantive directives — IDs, success criteria, and current evidence
paths — were extracted into `docs/DIRECTIVES.md` during the
`cycle-2026-05-09-doc-decomposition-and-wiring` realignment cycle. Status
language was reduced to a binary `open` / `done` and the prose register was
dropped. New directive work happens against `docs/DIRECTIVES.md`, not against
this archive.

The files preserved here are evidence-trail only. Do not edit them; do not
treat them as live policy. They remain readable for anyone tracing the
provenance of a directive ID, an audit packet, or a closeout decision.

## Files

- `CODEX.md` — the operating ledger as of revision 1.3 (2026-05-08).
- `HANDOFF.md` — the fresh-agent continuation prompt that paired with the codex.
- `HYDROLOGY.md` — VODA-aligned hydrology research notes.
- `VEGETATION_SOURCE_PIPELINE.md` — Pixel Forge vegetation pipeline notes.

The pre-codex 3,216-line evidence trail lives one level up at
`docs/archive/PROJEKT_OBJEKT_143_LEGACY.md` and is unchanged by this archive.
